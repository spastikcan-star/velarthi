About Game

Velarthi is a pixel art action-adventure game where you team up with your dragon to conquer enemy camps and collect precious rewards.

Game Features:

  • Epic dragon companion with unique abilities
  • Diverse enemy camps with increasing difficulty
  • Rewarding loot system with gold and equipment
  • Retro pixel art graphics with smooth animations
  • Progressive character development system
  • Challenging boss battles and secret areas

Story:

In a world where dragons and humans once lived in harmony, darkness has spread across the land. Enemy forces have established camps throughout the realm. You are the chosen one, bonded with the last remaining dragon, tasked with restoring peace by defeating these evil camps one by one.

How To Play

Controls:

  • WASD - Move your dragon
  • SPACE - Attack enemies
  • SHIFT - Special ability
  • E - Interact with objects

Tips:

  • Collect gold to upgrade your dragon
  • Watch enemy patterns before attacking
  • Use special abilities wisely
  • Explore every corner for hidden treasures

The Journey Ends

After conquering all enemy camps across the realm, you and your dragon companion face the ultimate challenge - the Dark Lord's fortress. This final battle will test everything you've learned on your journey.

Multiple Endings:

  • Hero's Victory - Defeat the Dark Lord with full health
  • Pyrrhic Victory - Win but lose your dragon companion
  • Secret Ending - Discover hidden truth about the darkness

Your choices throughout the game will determine which ending you receive. Will you bring lasting peace to the realm, or will darkness return?
